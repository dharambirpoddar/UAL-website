Home

We as a research group have the expertise in experimental fluid mechanics, Flow visualization, unsteady aerodynamics, and mathematical modeling aiming to unravel mysteries in fundamental fluid mechanics and solving problems in fire safety, cyclone-related natural disasters, efficient aerodynamic designs using biomimicry, and developing environment friendly novel wind turbines.

Our group brings together curious people from different geographical and cultural backgrounds. We value creativity, honesty, passion, and collaboration and we endeavor to provide a welcoming and inclusive work environment to tackle some of the challenging research problems together.
